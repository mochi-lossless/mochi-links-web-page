## 📌 How to preview locally

1. Put all files in the same folder.
2. Double-click `index.html` — it will open in your browser.
3. Check that images and links work!

---

## 🚀 How to publish for free

You can put your page online **for free** using:
- **GitHub Pages** *(recommended)*
- **Netlify**

---

### ✅ Option 1 — Deploy with GitHub Pages

1. **Create a GitHub account** (if you don’t have one).
2. **Create a new repository**, for example: `link-page`.
3. Upload all your files (`index.html`, `styles.css`, `profile.jpg`, `logo.png`).
4. Go to **Settings → Pages**.
5. Under **Source**, choose `branch: main` and `/ (root)`.
6. Click **Save** — GitHub will build your page.
7. After a minute, your page will be available at: https://yourusername.github.io/repositoryname/
8. Share your link in your bio!

---

### ✅ Option 2 — Deploy with Netlify (drag & drop)

1. Go to [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) and **create a free account**.
2. Click **Add new site → Deploy manually**.
3. Drag your folder with the 4 files to the upload box.
4. Netlify will generate a random link like: https://mochi-links.netlify.app/
5. Done! You can customize the link in the Netlify dashboard.

---

## ✨ Tips

- If you change your links or images, **edit `index.html`** and **upload again**.
- Make sure your images are **not too big**, to load fast.
- Keep a backup of this folder!
